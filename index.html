ABOUT
TODD SAYLOR

Todd Saylor is first and foremost a family
man who shares the word of God through
his Wired Differently movement. His
movement Wired Differently is Christ
centered. He is the author of several
books with the latest being “Your Will Be
Done.” He speaks all over the world
sharing his love for Christ and the purpose
of the Gospel. He shares his story of
receiving God’s favor, and how he was
receptive to the blessings he received. He
has put this movement above all of his
other accomplishments and wants to
share his story with you. This movement is
a discipleship that uses a biblical
foundation to teach and grow Christian
leaders, family members and business
owners.
